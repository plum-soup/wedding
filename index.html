Travel Logistics

Our Priorities

Lucy & JMei's foremost priority is that the people we love are there with us, spending quality time, and having lots of fun! We also love the outdoors, good food and drinks, and cute things (like kids & puppies).

Hence, we will be doing a few things to try and ensure a great time and an easy travel experience for all. Please help us by providing your details in our RSVP so that we can follow through.

Guest Rules

Every guest gets a +1 and as many kids and dogs as you're willing to bring. We will be arranging doggy and childcare during all wedding events as needed.

We will not be having a traditional bridal / groom party, nor have maid of hono(u)r or best man positions. We treasure all of our guests. Because of that, we are throwing out some of the roles and responsibilities to voluntary contributions. Please feel free to volunteer whatever you feel would be fun :)

Our wedding will be a no-gift wedding, your presence is present enough.

Accommodation

We will be providing lodge accommodations for all guests. There will be options for both more social arrangements vs more private ones and we will do our best to sort any shared rooms with existing friends.

Please note, we will assume that each guest and their +1 are good with sharing one bed.

If you want to arrange your own accommodation, or have needs that are difficult to accommodate, please note it on the RSVP. We will be reimbursing people up to a limit determined by everyone's accommodation costs (~$60 per person per night).

Getting to the Lodge

Our plan is to hire a bus to run between Denver airport / locations in Denver city to the lodge throughout Saturday, and back again throughout Monday. Closer to the date, we will ask for people's flight times to accommodate all arrivals and departures.

Flights from UK

We understand that the time zone changes and long haul flights from the UK are much more demanding than domestic US travel. To that effect, we will reimburse all UK guests $500 (~390 GBP) per flight seat needed.

Food and Drinks

We will be hosting dinners with drinks on both Saturday and Sunday. Light snacks and refreshments will be provided during the mixer and wedding prep time. Guests are responsible for all other meals and food. Please see the Location section for restaurant tips.

Other Travel Arrangements

Denver, the Rockies, and more broadly Colorado have been some of our favourite places to travel and explore! We highly encourage everyone, especially first-timers, to spend more time in the area, and have included some ideas and favo(u)rite places in the Location section.

Location tips

Video: Rocky Mountain National Park 🏔️

Some of our recommendations

Denver, CO

Chopstickers [Central Business District]

Chinese restaurant with soup dumplings.

Bang Up to the Elephant [Capitol Hill]

Caribbean food and a tiki bar. Warning: spicy.

Meow Wolf [Auraria]

Interactive art exhibit in a warehouse.

Larimer Square [Union Station]

Historic district where modern Denver was founded.

Central Denver Market [Curtis Park]

Gourmet market in artsy and brew-happy Rino district.

Estes Park, Colorado

Rocky Mountain National Park [1.5 hours drive from Lodge]

World class national park covering the most beautiful lakes and peaks of the Rockies. Particular favourites include Alberta Falls, Emerald Lake, and (if you're up for a challenge) Long's Peak, one of Colorado's many 14ers (over 14'000 ft elevation).

Scratch Deli & Bakery [8 min walk from Lodge]

Their killer breakfast burritos once saved our lives when we tried to hike during a winter storm.

Egg of Estes [33 mins walk from Lodge]

Diner serving all-American breakfast and lunch.

Coffee on the Rocks [12 mins walk from Lodge]

Coffee, need I say more?

Stanley Hotel [48 mins walk, 7 mins drive from Lodge]

The hotel that inspired Stephen King to write the Shining.

Colorado

Boulder, Colorado [1.5 hr drive from Denver]

Amazing hikes, rock climbing, and food!

Pikes Peak [2 hr drive from Denver]

Another Colorado 14er, but this one you can drive to the top.

Garden of the Gods [1 hr drive from Denver]

Like the name says, a red rocks adventureland.

Hot Sulphur Springs [2 hr drive from Denver]

Natural hot springs to soak off any hiking soreness.
