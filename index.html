Some of our recommendations

Denver, CO

Chopstickers [Central Business District]

Chinese restaurant with soup dumplings.

Bang Up to the Elephant [Capitol Hill]

Caribbean food and a tiki bar. Warning: spicy.

Meow Wolf [Auraria]

Interactive art exhibit in a warehouse.

Larimer Square [Union Station]

Historic district where modern Denver was founded.

Central Denver Market [Curtis Park]

Gourmet market in artsy and brew-happy Rino district.

Estes Park, Colorado

Rocky Mountain National Park [1.5 hours drive from Lodge]

World class national park covering the most beautiful lakes and peaks of the Rockies. Particular favourites include Alberta Falls, Emerald Lake, and (if you're up for a challenge) Long's Peak, one of Colorado's many 14ers (over 14'000 ft elevation).

Scratch Deli & Bakery [8 min walk from Lodge]

Their killer breakfast burritos once saved our lives when we tried to hike during a winter storm.

Egg of Estes [33 mins walk from Lodge]

Diner serving all-American breakfast and lunch.

Coffee on the Rocks [12 mins walk from Lodge]

Coffee, need I say more?

Stanley Hotel [48 mins walk, 7 mins drive from Lodge]

The hotel that inspired Stephen King to write the Shining.

Colorado

Boulder, Colorado [1.5 hr drive from Denver]

Amazing hikes, rock climbing, and food!

Pikes Peak [2 hr drive from Denver]

Another Colorado 14er, but this one you can drive to the top.

Garden of the Gods [1 hr drive from Denver]

Like the name says, a red rocks adventureland.

Hot Sulphur Springs [2 hr drive from Denver]

Natural hot springs to soak off any hiking soreness.
